Alex Fischman Cárdenas is a Peruvian director and producer whose award-winning short films have premiered at Sundance, Clermont-Ferrand, and Sitges, and earned Vimeo Staff Pick recognition. His commercial work has received nominations from the Cannes Young Director Award and Young Guns 23.
